# 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes **household electricity consumption patterns and energy efficiency opportunities** using data analytics and interactive dashboards.

The dashboards provide insights into how **appliance age, weather conditions, household size, and appliance type influence electricity usage**.

The objective is to help identify **high energy consumption drivers and design optimization strategies to reduce electricity usage and improve efficiency**.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Many households experience **high electricity bills due to inefficient energy usage patterns** caused by:

• Older appliances consuming excessive energy
• Weather conditions increasing cooling usage
• Peak hour electricity demand
• Inefficient appliance usage habits

Without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to **identify the main contributors to high electricity consumption and implement effective energy-saving strategies**.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to develop **interactive energy analytics dashboards** that:

• Monitor electricity consumption patterns
• Identify key drivers of energy usage
• Analyze the impact of appliance age on consumption
• Evaluate weather-related electricity demand
• Design and monitor energy optimization strategies

# 📊 Dashboard 1 — Electricity Consumption Diagnostic Insights

• Average Energy Consumption (Baseline)
• Total Energy Consumption
• Weather Impact on Energy

### Key Insights

# 📊 Dashboard 2 — Energy Reduction Strategy & Performance Monitoring

![Dashboard2](Dashboard/Optimization_Dashboard.jpeg)

### Strategic KPIs

• Adjusted Energy Consumption
• Energy Saved
• Average Optimized Energy
• Cooling Optimization Savings
• Efficiency Improvement Rate

### Optimization Insights

• Replacing appliances older than **5 years** significantly reduces energy usage.
• Cooling optimization contributes the largest portion of potential savings.
• Reducing peak hour electricity usage improves overall efficiency.
• Smart energy usage strategies can significantly lower monthly consumption.
